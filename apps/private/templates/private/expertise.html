Our Expertise

We are conscious of every possible risk in this incredibly complex market. Our platform reduces risk because it accounts for all laws that regulate dispensaries, improving the welfare of banks providing CIB banking services.

Cole Memo transformed the CIB banking industry

The Cole Memo is an extension of the Controlled Substances Act that prevents distribution of substances to minors, use of substances on federal property, money laundering, and many other illegal activities. Our software ensures that all activity abides with the entirety of the Cole Memo through detailed background checks with those purchasing, selling, and growing substances.

The Cole Memo aims to prevent:

  • Distribution to minors
  • Sale to criminals
  • Dispersion across state lines
  • Drug trafficking
  • Drug-related violence
  • Driving under the influence
  • Cultivation on public land
  • Use on federal property

BSA/AML pillars of service

  • 1     Designation of BSA compliance officer
  • 2     Development of internal policies, procedures and controls
  • 3     Ongoing, relevant training of employees
  • 4     Independent testing and review

Our platform complies with the pillars of the Bank Secrecy Act through our automatic SAR flagging system that will notify you of any non-compliant transactions. Our internal controls, training program, testing center, and designated officer BSA/AML officer all work together to prevent laundering.

Shield complies with both federal and state regulatory agencies

FinCEN

The Financial Crimes Enforcement Network creates the infrastructure for the enforcement of the critical Cole Memo. Since 2012, FinCEN has increased specificity of reporting, creating SARs and CTRs.

OFAC

The OFAC regulates international narcotics trafficking, including implementing elements of the Patriot Act and BSA/AML regulations.

Shield complies with many other industry standards...

HIPAA

The Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act safeguards healthcare incformation stored in a digital format. HIPAA covers nondisclosure and confidential data transmission standards.

KYC

Banks are liable for the illegal activities of MJ consumers. Shield’s enhanced due diligence measures ensure that our banks were blind to wrongdoing if prosecuted.

Patriot Act

The Patriot Act thwarts terrorism, but also includes measures to prevent international money laundering. The act amended existing BSA/AML laws and solidified the position of FinCEN.

BSA/AML

Anti-money laundering regulations are strict, but Shield has created a new level of transparency through our enhanced due diligence features at every step of the transaction tracking.

CSA

The Controlled Substances Act includes specific stipulations on the handling of controlled substances. Complying with the Cole Memo and CSA is critical to preventing illegal activity.

SAAE 16

SAAE 16 is an auditing standard for medical claims processors. Our audit material generator meets these standards, allowing for stress-free audit preparation.
