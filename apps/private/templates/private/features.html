One Click Compliance

Shield Compliance’s one-stop platform authenticates all purchases and transaction and tags them all for reference. We verify that the inventory of CIB’s matches with purchases made, cofirming that fraud isn’t a part of the equation.

Proactive Transparency

Our platform constantly monitors all transactions, and if we notice an inconsistency or potential risk, we both act and notify you immediately. You recieve automated alerts of tax liabilities and inventory discrepancies so that you can proceed to work with dispensaries in only the safest manner.

Additional Features

Shield Pay connects consumers to CIB locations via a secure ACH connection. ACH enables easy, repeated, cashless payments, adding convienance for both consumers and CIB locations. The Shield Pay application grants users a unique identifying pin number that allows them to pay at participating CIB locations
