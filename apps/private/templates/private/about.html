Our Vision

Shield Compliance minimizes risk using automation and network solutions for high-risk cash intensive businesses. Our clients see the immediate value of our comprehensive systems in enhancing bank and customer-end purchase experiences.

An idea

Since electronic networks transformed the financial markets, there’s no reason that compliance can’t be transformed by modern algorithms and computing innovations. Shield Compliance tackles old and new regulations with the latest technology, creating a heightened level of efficiency unapproached by existing compliance systems.


We make high-risk a concern of the past.

How we started

Our founders have lived and breathed this industry for over 30 years, gaining an insider-level of knowledge unmatched by other compliance companies. This industry experience makes our platform the perfectly designed product for banks’ and high-risk merchants’ needs.


We developed, refined and enhanced our platform for three years before coming to market. This period of work represents our detail-oriented nature and the extensiveness of the platform. Every feature is made to comply with the complex assortment of regulations surrounding high-risk banking.
